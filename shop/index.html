Work About Shop

Love Lychee is a pet project of mine where I sell some of my design creations on Etsy! The name comes from the legend of Yang Guifei, known as a beautiful consort of Emperor Xuanzong of Tang in ancient China, and her notorious love for lychees. I would love to expand my product line and include patches, stickers, and other decals!
